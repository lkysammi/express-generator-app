Sunday School Ministries

The goal of the Sunday school ministries is to raise up children in the knowledge of the Bible and love of God, and become full participants in congregational life. Excellent team of teachers look after the Sunday school ministries, conducted on every Sunday's at 9 - 10:15 AM. They follow relevant Bible based curriculum and contents are delivered to the children in the age group of 5 to 18.

Christian School Ministries

There is a Christian school run by the church, supported by the well-structured syllabus to run the grades from grade 1 to 8. They help students to aspire excellence in a life of learning, and discovering their God given uniqueness and role, anchored in God's word and Jesus Christ. Highly educated, well disciplined, strongly anointed believers of the Church are the teachers of the school.
